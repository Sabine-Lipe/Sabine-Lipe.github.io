The Center's Misson

At Hungry for Music, our mission is putting quality musical instruments into hungry hands. We serve children who demonstrate a desire to learn music, as well as teachers who have students willing to learn. We believe that by sharing instruments and musical experiences, children who would not otherwise have the opportunity can experience a kind of freedom and self-discovery that is often stifled in an atmosphere of economic hardship.

Who do we do it for

We support parents who cannot afford the cost of renting or purchasing an instrument. We support music teachers and program directors who have students unable to participate because of the cost. We support established, innovative community music programs that need support. We have served more than 16,000 children in the past 27 years.
